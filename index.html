yukiwang at cs.cornell.edu

Hi! I am a final year CS PhD candidate at Cornell Univeristy. I am currently a member of the PoRTaL lab, working with Prof. Sanjiban Choudhury. For Summer 2025, I am a Research Intern at Adobe, working with Stefano Petrangeli on AI assistants for content creation.

I work on interactive AI agents that learn and act given different levels of information (e.g., goal specification, vague language feedback, few-shot video demonstrations).

My research focuses on LLM agents, imitation learning (IRL), and foundation models for robotics.

I did my undergrad at Harvey Mudd College, where I did research in a wide range of areas: human-robot interactions(@HEATlab), underwater robotics (@LAIR), and experimental airplane construction (@HMC Aero Lab).
